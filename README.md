This repository showcases three data analytics projects demonstrating end-to-end data analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling skills. 
The Google Play Store Apps Analysis explores app ratings, downloads, pricing, and user behavior to identify factors driving app success and optimize app strategies.
The Hotel Booking Demand Analysis examines booking patterns, cancellations, guest demographics, and revenue metrics to enhance operational efficiency and improve
customer satisfaction in the hospitality industry. The Heart Health Analysis investigates patient demographics, clinical measurements, and medical history to
identify high-risk patients, uncover key risk factors, and provide actionable insights for early intervention and preventive care. Each project includes comprehensive data cleaning,
exploratory data analysis, metric development, and visualization, along with demonstrate practical applications
of Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and predictive modeling techniques.
